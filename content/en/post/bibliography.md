## Why Manage References?

Proper citations are essential in academic writing. Reference managers help you:

- Organize research papers
- Generate citations automatically
- Format bibliographies in any style (APA, MLA, Chicago, etc.)

## Popular Reference Managers

| Tool | Best for | Key Feature |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Zotero** | Free, open-source | Browser extension |
| **Mendeley** | Collaboration | PDF annotation |
| **EndNote** | Advanced users | Complex formatting |
| **Paperpile** | Google Docs users | Cloud-based |

## How to Add Citations to Your Website

With Hugo, you can write citations directly in Markdown:

Example: `(Dagne, 2024)`

Or use BibTeX format:

```bibtex
@article{example2024,
  title = {My Research Paper},
  author = {Dagne, Bereket},
  year = {2024}
}
